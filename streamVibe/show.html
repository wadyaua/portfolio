Description

Stranger Things is a sci-fi mystery series set in the small town of Hawkins, Indiana, where a group of kids uncover secret government experiments, supernatural forces, and a terrifying parallel dimension known as the Upside Down. When a young boy suddenly disappears, his friends, family, and the local police begin a search that leads them to a mysterious girl with extraordinary powers. As strange events continue to unfold, the group must face dangerous creatures, hidden conspiracies, and growing threats from another world. Across multiple seasons, the characters grow up together while battling increasingly powerful forces that threaten not only Hawkins, but the entire world.
