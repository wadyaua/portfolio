John Wick: Chapter 2

Legendary hitman John Wick is forced out of retirement when a former associate seeks his help in taking control of an international assassins’ guild. Bound by a blood oath, John travels to Rome where he faces the world’s most dangerous killers in a relentless fight for survival.

Description

John Wick is an action-packed thriller that follows a retired hitman who is forced back into the criminal underworld he once left behind. After a brutal act of violence shatters the last piece of peace in his life, John Wick sets out on a relentless path of vengeance. Known for his unmatched skills and deadly precision, he quickly becomes the most feared target and hunter at the same time. As he fights his way through waves of enemies, secrets of a hidden assassin society begin to unfold, revealing a world bound by strict codes, loyalty, and consequences.
